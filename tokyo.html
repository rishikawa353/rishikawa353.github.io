Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo, Japan is a very special place to me. Although I have never gotten to live there, I am fully Japanese, so I visit there all the time, and my whole family besides my parents lives there. One day, I would like the chance to live there, even if it is for a short period of time. The things I love most about Tokyo, Japan are the generosity of its people, the fantastic food even in 7/11s, and the overall architecture and ease of living.
