San Diego, California

My Hometown: San Diego is my hometown; specifically, I am from the west side of San Diego. San Diego is famous for its beautiful crystal-clear beaches, sea lions, good food, and boutique shopping places around the town. It is a pretty residential neighborhood and known to be a great retirement place. Hopefully, in the future, I will be able to return to San Diego and retire there.

Quick list

  • Sunset cliffs and ocean walks
  • Balboa Park and the zoo
  • Fish tacos and burritos
