Amalfi Coast

I got to visit the Amalfi Coast in the summer of 2023 after my high school graduation. It was a very special experience since it was my first time in Italy, and I got to spend time with my mom and dad. We stayed at a hotel with incredible views and amazing food, and the most memorable part of the trip was when we went on a boat together.

Highlights

  • Positano for colorful cliffside views
  • Amalfi for the cathedral and seaside walks
  • Ravello for gardens and music
