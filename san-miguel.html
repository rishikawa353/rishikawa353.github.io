San Miguel de Allende, Mexico

San Miguel de Allende was one of the most memorable trips that I have been on. Our flight did get delayed and took a long time to get there, but it was all worth it in the end. We got to walk through colonial streets, try great food, especially the seafood which was fantastic, and buy very cool souvenirs for my friends.

What stands out

Architecture
Colorful facades and historic churches.
Art
Local galleries and artisan markets.
Food
Street tacos and rooftop views.
